Breakfast

09:00 PM

Fresh summer avocado salad with eggs and peanuts

290 cal

Cooking time: 15 min

Proteins 21g

Carbs 22g

Fats 13g

Essential ingredients

  • Eggs – 1
  • Whole grain bread – 2 slices
  • Avocado – 1/3

Ingredients to your taste

  • Salt and pepper
  • Herbs

Preparations

  • 1. Boil a deep pot of water
  • 2. Turn off the heat and carefully crack the eggs into the pot. Cover for 5 min
  • 3. Toast the bread and smash the avocado on each piece of toast
  • 4. When the eggs are done, use a spatula to lift the eggs out of the water, and put them on top.
  • 5. Sprinkle with spices and herbs
